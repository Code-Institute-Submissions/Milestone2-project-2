Comfort

For those who have been working hard, looking for some RnR. Our Comfort holiday gives you a mix of activities, from walks, to shopping to just plain relaxation.

Adventure

For those who don't like to sit still. The Lake District offers a range of activities for the more active, from hiking, to cycling or even on the water, you might need another holiday after this one.

Country

Tired of the City? Escape to the Country. The holiday for those looking for a change of scenery and a different pace day to day. Relax, sample local produce and spend time enjoying not looking at the clock.
