Country Living

Enjoy a relaxing, week in the country.

Tired of the daily grind? Set out on the holiday that lets you push yourself, with a week of activities exploring the parts of the Lakes the average holidayer doesn't get to see.

Itinerary


Day 1

Your first day. Arrive at your cottage. Spend the time getting to know the local area, ready for the adventures ahead.

Day 2

Start your adventure with a cycle through and around the Lakes. Setting out early, you spend the day out on a gentle ride to get you used to the terrain... and the harder push tomorrow.

Day 3

A challenging day out, taking your bike you will go up an down a number of peaks in the Lakes.

Day 4

Active recovery is the agenda. Going for a walk, at a more leisurely pace you get to explore the towns in and around the Lakes.

Day 5

Uphill, uphill and more uphill. On your hike today, you will experience the most beautiful views the Lakes have to offer.

Day 6

Last day an easy one? Not a chance, go out onto the water, choosing between a range of activities from water-skiiing, open water swim or sailing.
